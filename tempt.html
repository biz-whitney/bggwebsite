WHO I WORK WITH

  • Brands and institutions who demonstrate that both sustainability as well as diversity, equity, and inclusion are at the core of their work
  • People who believe creativity, storytelling, and content creation are critical to create a collective culture shift to tackle the climate crisis
  • Schools, organizations, and thought leaders who want innovate ideas around climate solutions accessible and understandable to broader audiences

VALUE STATEMENT

As an Intersectional Environmentalist, I am excited to collaborate with brands and institutions who not only promote sustainability, but also address diversity, equity, and inclusion within their work. This includes, but is not limited to: the labor behind products, diversity of leadership/ decision-making within the brand, and the alignment of the brand’s values with Brown Girl Green.

SOME OF THE CONTENT I CREATE WITH BRANDS ARE:

  • IG LIVE recordings, IG static posts, and IG stories
  • Interview/ feature on the Brown Girl Green podcast and/or advertisement
  • Featured posts ranging from 300-1,500 words
  • Feature in Brown Girl Green’s weekly newsletter
